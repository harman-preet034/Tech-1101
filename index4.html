Difference between hobby and skill

Hobby in clear words is defined as any kind of activity which one does in their free time . Having hobbies is a great thing as it helps in reducing stress levels and fixes boredom . Nearly everyone in this world has hobbies which they love to do in their leisure time .

Skill on the other hand is a special ability to do a specific task or a sport . Generally , skills are developed by repetition and practice but skills may also be god gifted . For example , skills used for programming are developed by doing immense practice , whereas , the learning skill is mostly god gifted .
